# Values And Interests Example

## Core Values

- Shipping useful products over theoretical planning.
- Clear customer value.
- High ownership and direct communication.
- Data-informed decisions.
- Practical use of AI and automation.

## Work Style

- Prefer small teams with high responsibility.
- Comfortable working across product, engineering, design, data, and business.
- Like ambiguous problems when there is room to validate and iterate.

## Interests

- AI tools and agents
- FinTech and payments
- B2B SaaS
- Developer tools
- Data products
- Marketplaces

## Company Preferences

- Clear product direction.
- Strong technical team.
- Remote-friendly or compatible location.
- Users or customers with real pain points.
