# Person Summary

At Blockscout, he leads product strategy for privacy and cross-chain infrastructure, including zk-based flows and EVM explorer capabilities. His work has delivered measurable growth outcomes, including 350% YoY TVL growth, 300% MAU growth from ecosystem onboarding initiatives, and 280% MAU growth with a partner-driven rewards framework, while also improving long-term retention.

Across advisory and leadership roles at Saola, Mine’d, Tiggy, and Prisma Labs, he has repeatedly driven monetization and growth through experimentation, product architecture, and lifecycle optimization, with results such as ARR growth, doubled sales after new product launch, increased ARPU/revenue, and stronger conversion and activation performance.

In parallel with his product leadership work, he builds open-source Web3 projects (Hush, Mycelium SDK, Growthcast) focused on privacy-preserving DeFi, account abstraction, and ecosystem analytics. He also contributes publicly as a speaker at events including EthCC and DappCon, and as an author on blockchain privacy and compliance.
